## Cultural / art‑house cinemas

Besides the commercial multiplexes, Medellín has cultural centers with dedicated cinema auditoriums. [colombomedellin.edu](https://www.colombomedellin.edu.co/cine/)

- **Teatro MAMM (Museo de Arte Moderno de Medellín)** – One or more cinema halls inside MAMM, programming art, independent, and festival cinema. [elmamm](https://www.elmamm.org/cine/)
- **Centro Colombo Americano de Medellín** – Two cinema halls at the Centro headquarters (Cra 45 #53‑24, “El Palo con Maracaibo”), operating as an art/independent cinema venue. [colombomedellin.edu](https://www.colombomedellin.edu.co/cine/)
- Various smaller cineclubes and temporary venues also exist (universities, cultural houses), but they are not always continuously active or listed on commercial showtime portals. [cinefagos](https://www.cinefagos.net/libros/SALAS-DE-CINE-Y-CINECLUBES-EN-MEDELLIN.pdf)

## How complete is this list?

- The **Colombia.com Medellín cinema index** enumerates the main mall‑based complexes (Arkadia, Aventura, Bosque Plaza, El Tesoro, Florida, La Central, Las Américas, Molinos, Monterrey, Multicine La 65, Oviedo, Premium Plaza, Santafé, Terminal del Sur). [colombia](https://www.colombia.com/cine/medellin-c9)
- Local guides such as **Medellin Guru**, **Medellín Living**, and **El Colombiano’s coverage** confirm these and add the metro‑area locations Mayorca, Puerta del Norte, Aves María, City Plaza, Viva Envigado, and the cultural theaters MAMM and Centro Colombo Americano. [terryandresen.blogspot](http://terryandresen.blogspot.com/2015/02/medellin-movie-theater-guide.html)

Because smaller cineclubs and occasional screening rooms open and close or do short‑run series, no public source guarantees a 100% exhaustive list at every moment.  For practical purposes (commercial showtimes + stable cultural venues), the theaters listed above represent essentially all regularly operating movie theaters in Medellín and its immediate metro area. [cinefagos](https://www.cinefagos.net/libros/SALAS-DE-CINE-Y-CINECLUBES-EN-MEDELLIN.pdf)
